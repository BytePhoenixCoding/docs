## Free tier

- Immediate access to the LaunchSniper, MultiSniper, and SolanaSniper bots with essential settings.
- Great for experimenting with strategies or monitoring how the automations behave.
- A **1% trading volume fee** is collected automatically whenever your unpaid balance crosses the internal threshold.
- Upgrade is available at any time without losing your transaction history.

## Pro tier

- Unlocks the complete feature set, including trailing stop-loss, advanced channel filters, liquidity pair controls, and priority access to future tooling.
- **0% performance fee** on profitable trades—what you make is what you keep.
- Dedicated options for configuring custom RPC nodes, automation guards, and premium monitoring utilities.

### Access options

| Option | What you get | Ideal for |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Lifetime purchase** | One payment for permanent Pro access. | Power users who rely on BTS daily. |
| **Subscription** | Recurring monthly access that stays active while the plan is paid. | Traders who want flexibility or shorter-term use. |

<Note>
  Specific pricing, renewal dates, and upgrade actions are always shown inside the Telegram interface so you can confirm the latest terms before paying.
</Note>

## Referral boosts

- Share your link from the bot's **Referral** panel to lower onboarding friction for friends or communities.
- Referred users receive a **10% discount** when they subscribe or buy Pro, and you earn **20%** commission on the tier's full price plus **25%** of their collected trading fees.
- Payouts and accumulated balances can be reviewed and claimed right inside the bot.

Learn more in the full [referral system guide](/referral-system).

## Managing your membership

1. Open [`@btsv3bot`](https://t.me/btsv3bot) and run `/start`.
2. Choose **My Account → Tier & Billing** to see your current status, renewal date, and historical payments.
3. Purchase, renew, or cancel from that same menu—no extra site or download required.

<Note>
  For legacy lifetime holders from V1/V2, check the [migration guide](/migration) to see how your historical tier maps to V3 benefits.
</Note>

BlockchainTokenSniper V3 includes a fully automated referral engine managed inside [`@btsv3bot`](https://t.me/btsv3bot). Every user can generate a personalized link and start earning within minutes.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Grab your referral link">
    Open the bot, navigate to **Referral** or **My Account → Referral**, and copy the URL (`https://t.me/btsv3bot?start=YOUR_REFERRAL_ID`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Share it anywhere">
    Send the link to friends, post it in communities, or include it in your alpha channel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Let the bot track everything">
    As soon as someone starts the bot through your link, they are tied to your account for discounts and payouts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Self-referrals are ignored. Trying to use your own referral ID will not generate extra benefits.
</Note>

## Referral benefits

- **Discounts for new users** – Referrals automatically receive a **10%** discount when they subscribe or buy a tier.
- **Tier purchase commissions** – You earn **20%** of the original (pre-discount) tier price. Payouts are sent automatically once the purchase is confirmed.
- **Trading fee revenue share** – Earn **25%** of the trading fees generated by your referred users. These balances accumulate and can be claimed in-app.

## Managing referrals and payouts

Inside the Telegram bot you can:

- View or regenerate your referral link.
- Adjust discount percentages when that feature is enabled for your account.
- Track the number of active referrals and lifetime earnings.
- Review historical payouts and current claimable trading-fee balances.
- Trigger withdrawals of accumulated fees directly to your configured wallet.

<Note>
  Program percentages or rules may change over time. Always check the Referral panel in the bot for the most current terms before promoting your link.
</Note>
